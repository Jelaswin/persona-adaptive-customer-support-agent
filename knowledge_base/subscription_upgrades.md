## Upgrade Process

### Free to Pro
1. Go to Dashboard > Settings > Subscription.
2. Click "Upgrade to Pro."
3. Select monthly ($29/mo) or annual ($290/yr — save 2 months).
4. Enter payment details.
5. Confirmation email sent within 5 minutes.

### Pro to Enterprise
Enterprise plans are custom-priced. Contact the sales team:
- Email: enterprise@adsparkx.ai
- Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
- In-app: Dashboard > Settings > Contact Sales

## Proration
- Upgrades are prorated for the remainder of the current billing cycle.
- The prorated amount is calculated automatically at checkout.
- No refunds on downgrades; credit is applied to future invoices.

## API Rate Limits by Tier
- Free: 1,000 requests/day, 10 requests/minute.
- Pro: 50,000 requests/day, 100 requests/minute.
- Enterprise: Custom rate limits.
