# Password Reset Guide

## Overview
Users can reset their AdSparkX account password through the self-service portal or by contacting support.

## Self-Service Password Reset
1. Navigate to the login page at https://app.adsparkx.ai/login
2. Click "Forgot Password?" below the login form.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Check your inbox for a password reset email (subject: "Reset your AdSparkX password").
5. Click the reset link in the email. The link expires in 60 minutes.
6. Enter your new password. Requirements:
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter
   - At least one lowercase letter
   - At least one number
   - At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
7. Confirm the new password and submit.

## Troubleshooting
- **Email not received**: Check spam/junk folder. Whitelist no-reply@adsparkx.ai. Wait up to 5 minutes.
- **Expired link**: Request a new reset link. Links expire after 60 minutes for security.
- **Account locked**: After 5 failed reset attempts, accounts are locked for 30 minutes.

## Contact Support
If self-service reset fails, contact support with your registered email and account ID.
