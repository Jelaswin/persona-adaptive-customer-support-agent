# Account Lockouts Guide

## Why Accounts Get Locked

### 1. Multiple Failed Login Attempts
- 5 consecutive failed login attempts → 30-minute lockout.
- 10 consecutive failed attempts across 24 hours → 24-hour lockout.
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset and automatically unlock.

### 2. Suspicious Activity Detection
- Login from a new geographic location.
- Multiple failed API authentication attempts.
- Unusual usage pattern detected.
- Lockout duration varies (typically 1-24 hours).

### 3. Payment Failure (3+ attempts)
- After 3 failed payment attempts, the account is restricted.
- Access is restored once payment is successful.

### 4. Terms of Service Violation
- Accounts may be locked pending investigation.
- Contact support for details.

## Unlocking Your Account

### Self-Service Unlock
1. Go to the login page.
2. Click "Forgot Password."
3. Reset your password.
4. Log in with the new password.

### Automatic Unlock
Lockouts from failed logins are automatically lifted after the lockout period expires.

### Contact Support
For lockouts not resolved by self-service:
- Email: support@adsparkx.ai
- Include: Account email, account ID, reason for lockout (if known).

## Prevention
- Use a password manager to avoid incorrect passwords.
- Enable two-factor authentication for added security.
- Keep your account recovery email up to date.
