# Refund Policy

## 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
AdSparkX offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on all new subscriptions.

## Eligibility
- New subscriptions are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Annual plan refunds are prorated after the first 30 days.
- Add-ons and one-time purchases are non-refundable.
- Renewals are non-refundable.

## Refund Process
1. Submit a refund request via Dashboard > Settings > Billing > Request Refund.
2. Provide the reason for cancellation.
3. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.
4. The refund is credited to the original payment method.
5. You will receive a confirmation email once processed.

## Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued if:
- Service was interrupted for more than 24 hours.
- A feature promised in your plan was unavailable.
- The issue is on AdSparkX's side.

## Non-Refundable Items
- Domain name registrations.
- Third-party integration fees.
- Setup fees (if applicable).
- Overages on API usage beyond plan limits.

## Cancellation
Canceling your subscription stops future billing but does not automatically trigger a refund. Submit a separate refund request if eligible.
