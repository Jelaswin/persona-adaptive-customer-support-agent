# Email Verification Guide

## Why Verify Your Email
Email verification is required to:
- Activate your AdSparkX account.
- Receive password reset links.
- Receive billing notifications.
- Receive service status updates.

## Verification Process
1. Create an account at https://app.adsparkx.ai/signup.
2. A verification email is sent to the address you provided.
3. Click the verification link in the email (expires in 24 hours).
4. Your account is activated immediately.

## Resending Verification Email
1. Go to https://app.adsparkx.ai/resend-verification.
2. Enter your email address.
3. A new verification email is sent within 2 minutes.
4. Check spam if not received.

## Common Issues

### Email Not Received
- Check spam/junk folder.
- Add no-reply@adsparkx.ai to your contacts.
- Check for typos in your email address.
- Try a different email provider (Gmail, Outlook, etc.).
- Wait 5 minutes — some providers delay delivery.

### Expired Verification Link
Verification links expire after 24 hours. Request a new verification email.

### Already Verified
If you receive a "already verified" message, you can log in directly.

## Changing Your Email
1. Log in to Dashboard.
2. Go to Settings > Account.
3. Enter the new email address.
4. A verification email is sent to the new address.
5. Both old and new emails receive a notification of the change.
