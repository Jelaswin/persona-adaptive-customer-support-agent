# Billing Disputes Guide

## Common Billing Issues

### 1. Double Charge
If you were charged twice for the same subscription:
- Check your billing history in Dashboard > Settings > Billing.
- Verify both charges are not pre-authorization holds (temporary holds are released within 5 business days).
- If confirmed double charge: contact support with transaction IDs.

### 2. Incorrect Amount Charged
- Verify your current plan and any add-ons in Dashboard.
- Check for prorated charges from mid-cycle upgrades.
- Sales tax may vary by location (added automatically).

### 3. Unexpected Charges
Possible reasons:
- Automatic renewal after trial period ended.
- Add-on or overage charges.
- Team member seat addition.
- Currency conversion fees (if applicable).

### 4. Dispute Process
1. Check billing history in Dashboard.
2. Contact support with the charge date, amount, and transaction ID.
3. Allow 2-3 business days for investigation.
4. If unresolved, email disputes@adsparkx.ai with full details.

## Chargebacks
- Chargebacks result in immediate account suspension.
- A $25 chargeback fee applies (covers processing costs).
- To resolve, contact support to reverse the chargeback.

## Invoice Requests
- Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month.
- Download invoices from Dashboard > Settings > Billing > Invoices.
- Custom invoicing is available for Enterprise plans.
