# Payment Failures Guide

## Common Causes of Payment Failure

### 1. Insufficient Funds
The card issuer declined the transaction due to insufficient funds.
- Use a different payment method.
- Contact your bank to increase your limit.

### 2. Card Expired
The credit/debit card on file has expired.
- Update your payment method with a valid card.
- Check the expiration date on your card.

### 3. Incorrect Card Details
- Billing address must match what the bank has on file.
- CVV code must be entered correctly.
- Card number must be entered without spaces or dashes.

### 4. Bank Decline (No Specific Reason)
Some banks decline transactions for security reasons.
- Contact your bank to authorize the transaction.
- Try a different card or payment method.

### 5. Payment Gateway Timeout
The payment gateway did not respond in time.
- Wait 5 minutes and retry.
- Check https://status.adsparkx.ai for any ongoing issues.

## What Happens After Failure
- Your subscription remains active for the current billing period.
- A retry is attempted automatically after 3 and 7 days.
- After 3 failed attempts, your account is downgraded to the free tier.
- You will receive email notifications for each failed attempt.

## Updating Payment Method
1. Go to Dashboard > Settings > Billing.
2. Click "Update Payment Method."
3. Enter new card details.
4. Click "Save."
5. A $1.00 temporary hold will be placed for verification and released within 5 business days.
