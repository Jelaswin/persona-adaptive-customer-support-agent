# User Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to AdSparkX!
This guide walks you through the first steps to get started with AdSparkX.

## Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Go to https://app.adsparkx.ai/signup.
2. Enter your name, email, and create a password.
3. Verify your email (check inbox for verification link).
4. Complete your profile (company name, role, use case).

## Step 2: Explore the Dashboard
- **Overview**: Key metrics, recent activity, quick actions.
- **Analytics**: Reports, charts, and data export.
- **Integrations**: Connect your tools (databases, CRMs, etc.).
- **Settings**: Account, billing, team management.

## Step 3: Connect Your First Integration
1. Go to Dashboard > Integrations.
2. Choose your data source (database, API, file upload).
3. Follow the setup wizard.
4. Test the connection.
5. Start importing data.

## Step 4: Create Your First Workflow
1. Go to Dashboard > Workflows.
2. Click "New Workflow."
3. Choose a template (Data Sync, Alert, Report, etc.).
4. Configure triggers and actions.
5. Test and activate.

## Step 5: Invite Your Team
1. Go to Dashboard > Settings > Team.
2. Click "Invite Member."
3. Enter their email and role (Admin, Editor, Viewer).
4. They will receive an invitation email.
5. Team members appear in your team list within 5 minutes.
