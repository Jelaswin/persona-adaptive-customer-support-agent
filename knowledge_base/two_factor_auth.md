# Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Guide

## Overview
Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your AdSparkX account. Even if your password is compromised, 2FA prevents unauthorized access.

## Supported Methods

### 1. Authenticator App (Recommended)
Supports any TOTP-based authenticator app:
- Google Authenticator
- Microsoft Authenticator
- Authy
- 1Password (with 2FA support)
- LastPass Authenticator

### 2. SMS Codes
A 6-digit code sent to your registered phone number.
- Standard carrier rates may apply.
- SMS delivery is not guaranteed in all regions.

### 3. Backup Codes
- 10 one-time backup codes are generated when 2FA is enabled.
- Each code can be used once.
- Store codes securely (print or save offline).
- If you lose access, backup codes are your only recovery option.

## Enabling 2FA
1. Go to Dashboard > Settings > Security.
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication."
3. Choose your method (Authenticator App recommended).
4. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app.
5. Enter the 6-digit code from your app to confirm.
6. Save your 10 backup codes.

## Disabling 2FA
1. Log in to your account.
2. Go to Dashboard > Settings > Security.
3. Click "Disable Two-Factor Authentication."
4. Enter your current password to confirm.

## Recovery Without 2FA
If you lose access to both your 2FA method and backup codes:
1. Contact support and verify your identity.
2. Provide: account email, account ID, and proof of identity (government ID).
3. 2FA is removed within 1-2 business days after verification.
