# Service Outages & Incident Management

## Status Page
Real-time service status: https://status.adsparkx.ai
Subscribe to updates via email or Slack.

## Incident Types

### 1. Partial Outage
- Some features are affected.
- Core functionality remains available.
- Resolution typically within 1-4 hours.
- Example: Analytics dashboard loading slowly.

### 2. Major Outage
- Core service is unavailable.
- All features are impacted.
- Resolution typically within 4-12 hours.
- Example: API endpoint returning 503 errors.

### 3. Degraded Performance
- Service is operational but slower than normal.
- Response times > 2 seconds (normal: < 200ms).
- Resolution typically within 1-2 hours.

## Communication During Outages
- Status page is updated every 30 minutes during incidents.
- Email notifications sent for Major Outages.
- Post-incident report (PIR) published within 5 business days.
- PIR includes: root cause, resolution timeline, and preventive measures.

## Compensation
- Pro plans: 1 day credit for every 2 hours of downtime (max 7 days).
- Enterprise plans: As per SLA agreement.
- Submit compensation requests within 30 days of the incident.
